About Us

Founded by Mr. Foday Kebbeh, Kebbeh Agricultural Farm is dedicated to producing quality crops and livestock while promoting sustainable agriculture and empowering rural youth in The Gambia.

Mission

To produce quality agricultural products while empowering local communities through sustainable farming practices.

Vision

A self-reliant farming community where agriculture creates prosperity for all.

  • Hard work and honesty
  • Sustainability and innovation
  • Community development
  • Youth empowerment

Our Activities

Crop Production

We grow maize, groundnuts, rice, and vegetables, providing chemical-free produce to local markets and households.

Livestock & Poultry

We raise goats, sheep, and poultry with proper care to ensure healthy and high-quality livestock.

Water & Irrigation

Our water initiative in Wuli Jah Kunda improves access to clean water for farming and domestic use.
